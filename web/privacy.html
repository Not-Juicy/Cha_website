Privacy Policy

How the Cambodian Haemophilia Association collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: June 2026

This is a placeholder policy. CHA will review and finalise this document with legal counsel before the public site goes live. In the meantime, the principles below describe how we handle information today.

1. Who we are

The Cambodian Haemophilia Association (CHA) is a patient-led non-profit organisation supporting people with bleeding disorders across Cambodia. Our registered office is in Phnom Penh. Throughout this policy, "we", "us", and "our" refer to CHA.

2. Information we collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services and support our community. This may include:

  • Membership information — name, contact details, date of birth, and (with consent) medical information relevant to your bleeding disorder.
  • Donation information — name, contact details, and payment information processed securely by Stripe or PayPal. We do not store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Website analytics — basic, anonymised usage data to help us improve the site.
  • Communications — when you email us, fill in a form, or join our Telegram or Facebook groups.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide patient support, education, and community programs.
  • Process memberships and donations.
  • Communicate with you about CHA events, news, and updates (you can opt out at any time).
  • Improve our website and services.

4. Sharing your information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information only with:

  • Treatment centres and partner organisations, with your consent.
  • Service providers (e.g. payment processors, hosting) who are bound by confidentiality and data-protection obligations.
  • Authorities, when required by law.

5. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@chacambodia.org.kh.

6. Data security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Donation transactions are processed via Stripe and PayPal using encrypted connections; CHA never stores full payment card details.

7. Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies for site functionality and minimal analytics cookies to understand how visitors use the site. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Children

Our services support people of all ages, including children. We take extra care with information about minors and require parental or guardian consent for membership.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our handling of your data, please contact us:

  • Address: Phnom Penh, Cambodia
